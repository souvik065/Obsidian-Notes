# What to do about it (short, practical next steps)

1. **Validate the grief.** Name it: “I grieve the future I imagined.” Let the feeling exist for a time.
    
2. **Contain the pain.** Use the 10-minute toolbox: grounding breath, 5-4-3-2-1 sensory reset, and a short physical interrupt.
    
3. **Create an outlet.** Write an unsent letter (pour everything out). Then archive or ritualize letting it go.
    
4. **Rebalance investment.** Stop one behavior that keeps the ledger active (checking statuses, monthly calls, doing favors that aren’t reciprocated).
    
5. **Redirect the energy.** Convert that devotion into one focused project: a research milestone, a fitness goal, or a creative piece. Make measurable progress for 90 days.
    
6. **Test reality later.** If you meet her again, show the new, calm self — not explained change. Behavior rewrites impressions, not speeches.
    
7. **Get support if needed.** A therapist, mentor, or trusted friend speeds recovery and prevents relapse into attachment patterns.
